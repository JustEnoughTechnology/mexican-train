## Design Principles

### **Accessibility First**
- ✅ High contrast ratios for all colors against light background
- ✅ Colors distinguishable for most types of colorblindness
- ✅ No reliance on color alone - numbers still visible
- ✅ Professional game appearance

### **Visual Clarity**
- ✅ Each color is distinctly different from neighbors
- ✅ No similar shades that could be confused
- ✅ Clear visibility at small domino size (40x82 pixels)
- ✅ Works well on nearly-white background

### **Color Distribution**
- **Blues**: Royal Blue (1), Teal (7) - separated for distinction
- **Greens**: Forest Green (2) - single green shade
- **Purples**: Purple (5), Dark Purple (12) - different intensities
- **Warm Colors**: Dark Orange (4), Gold (6), Burnt Orange (10)
- **Dark Colors**: Dark Gray (8), Black (11), Maroon (9)
- **Special**: Light Gray (0) for blanks, Deep Magenta (3) for accent

## Background Recommendation
- **Suggested Background**: `#faf9f2` (warm off-white)
- **Current**: Red background causes poor legibility
- **Future**: Light cream/ivory background for optimal contrast
